Hi there! 😎✨  I am a PhD student in Education at the University of California, Irvine, advised by <a href="http://niadowell.com/">Dr. Nia Nixon</a> and <a href="https://markwarschauer.com/">Dr. Mark Warschauer</a>.

My research is at the intersection of Educational Data Science, Human-AI Teaming, and Collaboration Analytics:

- **Educational Data Science**: How can Machine Learning and NLP help us measure and improve learning?
- **Human-AI Teaming**: When AI becomes a teammate to solve complex problems together, how do group dynamics shift?
- **Collaboration Analytics & Inclusion Analytics**: How can we measure complex sociocognitive dynamics — such as sense of belonging and inclusion — in collaborative learning using NLP?
